   - Measure the input voltage (\(V_{in}\)) without the series resistor.

   - Output impedance (\(Z_{out}\)) can be calculated as \(Z_{out} = R_L \times \frac{\Delta V}{\Delta I}\), where \(\Delta V = V_{oc} - V_{load}\).
